About Audi

```

Audi was founded on July 16, 1909, by August Horch in Germany. The name Audi is the Latin translation of Horch, which means "listen" in German. Over more than a century, Audi has become one of the world's most respected luxury automobile manufacturers. The company is known for innovation, premium engineering, outstanding performance, and groundbreaking technologies such as the famous quattro all-wheel-drive system. Today Audi continues to shape the future of mobility through advanced electric vehicles, sustainable engineering, and world-class automotive design.
